The stack

An in-house, context-aware
capture loop.

The pendant is the smallest part of the story. Everything that gives the product its value is software we own, end to end.

01
Capture

Picks up ambient audio from any clean mic at the bedside.

02
Understand

In-house AI that understands clinical speech, abbreviations, and medical terms out of the box.

03
Resolve

Identifies primary keys from the conversation: doctor, patient, schedule, ward, bed, and the full encounter context.

04
Structure

Chunks each conversation segment by patient and encounter type: OPD consult, IPD round, observations, plans.

05
Write

Copies into the EMR form, ready to save and print.

The pipeline

Same loop for
every room.

The system doesn't care which role you play. It identifies the clinician, the encounter, and the right place in the EMR. The clinician doesn't think about OPD versus IPD — that is the system's job.

01 · Doctor
OPD consult or IPD round.

OPD consultations, IPD rounds for clinical notes, consultant notes, and OT notes.

02 · Nurse / MO
Intake, observations, bedside checks.

Progress notes, admission assessment forms, and routine nursing documentation.

03 · Anyone, really
One pipeline. Every encounter.

The same in-house stack works wherever a clinician speaks and an EMR field needs filling.
